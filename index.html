Privacy Policy for Don't Say

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Introduction

Don't Say ("we", "our", or "us") is a party word game app developed by Gidi Vigo. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, including:

  • App interaction data (screen views, button taps, game events)
  • Device information and identifiers
  • App performance and diagnostics data

This data is collected automatically and does not include personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

How We Use Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Understand how users interact with the app
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Analytics data is processed by Google Firebase in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

Data Security

All data transmitted from the app is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

User Accounts

Don't Say does not require or support user accounts. No login or registration is needed to use the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
